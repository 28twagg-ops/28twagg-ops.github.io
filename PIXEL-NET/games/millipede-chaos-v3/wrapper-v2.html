Millipede Chaos (version 3.0)

HOW TO PLAY

Move: Arrow Keys / WASD (mobile: drag)
Fire: Space (mobile: tap/hold)
Goal: Clear millipede segments, survive spiders/bees, and climb waves.
Initials come from PIXEL-NET (no in-game prompt). Badge is clickable to change.
Game Over: score auto-saves.
